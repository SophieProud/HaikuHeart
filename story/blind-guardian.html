Modern Bards, the Blind Guardians


High Fantasy of the Metal Kind

When you survive three decades and ten albums, when you become the epitome of everything fantasy, when your songs are able of sending 50k people in a chanting fury, that is called power.

Blind Guardian's best known song, The Bard's Song, is living proof that the classical style of storytelling songs are still capable of eliciting honest responses from a modern audience.



Such intense passion has made for the most amazing concerts in the world, when the audience sang "Valhalla, deliverance/Why have you ever forgotten me" over and over again, with the band bewildered, humbled and unsure of how to deal with such sudden devotion.



This is still the best moment of all times.

For more incredible music from Blind Guardian, you can listen to them through Spotify, Youtube and anywhere else in the world with music. Seriously, these guys are crazy good and popular.
