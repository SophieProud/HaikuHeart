Haiku Collection

In haiku, authors don't matter. If you are able to see what they write about, that's all that matter.

緑岩<
村に帰依ってしまった
森へ探す
The green stones
Have disappeared from the village.
Search now in the forest.
Twig that hit the log
And awoke the sunset's God
Only I heard you.
小さい枝
ムザムザ泣いた
蛇ように
A tiny twig
Cried silently
Like a viper
川女神
冷たいですかね？
手の中に。
Godess of the river
You are cold
In my hands, aren't you?
雪の花
風も私も
息を止める
The flowers of snow.
The wind and I
Both hold our breaths.
凍って天使
羽わ棚引く
僕の頭の上
Frozen angel
His feathers hover
Above my head.
